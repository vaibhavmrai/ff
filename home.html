About Us

NITK Films Club centered at National Institute of Technology- Karnataka, Surathkal has been screening the best of Indian and International Cinema in the campus for the past thirty years. In our debut venture, The Film Festival aimed at celebrating the best of Cinema and encouraging young filmmakers to bring out the storytellers in them. This year Films' Club NITK is proud to present the Fifth edition of the NITK Film Festival which is aimed to bring you the best of cinema in 3 days filled with excitement, fun and amazing experiences you won't forget!
